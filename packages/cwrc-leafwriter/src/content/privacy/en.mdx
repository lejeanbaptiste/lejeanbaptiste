## Data Collection and Storage

### What data is collected

LEAF-Writer collects data for four main purposes:

- **Necessary information** _(required)_ for the proper functioning of the website. For instance, we collect and store your browser's language, colour theme preferences, lookup preferences, configurations for custom schema and custom authorities, etc.
- **Error monitoring** _(required)_ to maintain site reliability and improve user experience. We use a third-party error monitoring service to detect and diagnose technical issues. This service captures information about errors and application behaviour only. No personal data is transmitted to or stored by this service or by LEAF-Writer.

### How Data is Stored

LEAF-Writer stores your data on your browser using a combination of local storage and IndexedDB. You can inspect them at any time using your browser's developer tools.

Personal data we process for any purpose(s) shall not be kept longer than is necessary for the purpose(s) for which it is collected. The duration varies by purpose and location. Information stored in your local storage and IndexedDB is kept indefinitely.
